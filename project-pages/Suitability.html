Suitability Analysis
Project Details
I was assigned to perform a suitability analysis on the city of Stowe, Vermont, and identify the best locations for a new landfill site. For this task, I had to consider the following criteria in order to find a site:

- Locations with flatter slopes are more suitable
- Locations farthest from streams are more suitable
- Locations farthest from existing landfills are more suitable
