About Me
I was raised in The Twin Cities, MN and am currently finishing my final semester at The University of Wisconsin-Madison. In May, I will be graduating with a degree in Landscape & Urban Studies and a certificate in Environmental Sciences.
I was introduced to GIS through in introductory course during my sophomore year. Since then, I have developed a strong understanding of industry standard softwares (ArcGISPro, QGIS, ENVI) and how to effectively use them to analyze and visualize geospatial data. I plan on pursuing a career in GIS, with a focus in equitable and sustainable urban environments. You'll find page links to my projects below, enjoy!
